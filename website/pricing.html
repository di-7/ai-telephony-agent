What does the ₹1,500 setup fee cover?

The setup fee covers AI agent discovery, custom voice and script training, phone number provisioning, CRM integration, and full onboarding. Your AI agent will be tailored specifically to your business.

How does the free trial month work?

After paying the ₹1,500 setup fee, you get a full month of unlimited access to our platform at no extra cost. Use all features, make unlimited calls, and see real results before committing further.

What happens after the free trial?

If you're satisfied with the performance, simply pay the remaining ₹3,500 to continue your service. After that, the plan renews at ₹5,000/month. If you're not satisfied, you can walk away — no further charges.

Is the ₹1,500 refundable if I don't continue?

The setup fee covers real costs — AI training, custom configuration, and phone provisioning done specifically for your business — so it is non-refundable. However, you get a full month free to validate the value before paying anything more.

How quickly can I get started?

Most businesses are live within 48 hours of paying the setup fee. We handle discovery, AI agent training, phone number setup, and integration with your existing systems.

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Yes! For larger organizations with specific needs, we offer custom enterprise plans with additional features, dedicated infrastructure, and tailored pricing. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
